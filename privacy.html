Important: This policy complies with Apple App Store requirements, GDPR, and CCPA. By using ChordCrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Introduction

ChordCrack ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ChordCrack ("the App").

Data Controller: ChordCrack is the data controller for your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Required for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Username: Your chosen display name within the App
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (we never store plain text passwords)
  • Apple Sign-In Information: When using Apple Sign-In, we receive your Apple ID, name (if shared), and email address (real or private relay)

2.2 Game and Usage Data

  • Performance Statistics: Scores, accuracy rates, streak records, completion times
  • Game Sessions: Date, duration, chord types practiced, and results
  • Achievements and Progress: Unlocked milestones and skill level progression
  • App Preferences: Settings, privacy choices, and user interface preferences

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Technical Data: IP address (for security), crash reports, performance metrics
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, session duration, navigation patterns (anonymized)
  • Biometric Authentication Data: Face ID/Touch ID authentication status (stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to our servers)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to provide and improve our App:

3.1 Supabase (Database and Authentication)

  • Purpose: Secure cloud database for storing your game data and account information
  • Data Shared: Account information, game statistics, user preferences
  • Location: Data processed in secure data centers (primarily US and EU)
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Data Protection: GDPR compliant with appropriate data processing agreements

3.2 Apple Services

  • Apple Sign-In: For secure authentication with privacy-focused features
  • iCloud Sync: For backing up your preferences and settings (if enabled)
  • App Store Analytics: Basic app performance and crash reporting

4. How We Use Your Information

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR):

  • Contract Performance: To provide app services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve app performance and security
  • Consent: For marketing communications (opt-in only)

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable account functionality, save progress, sync across devices
  • Personalize Experience: Track achievements, maintain progress, provide personalized recommendations
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Ensure Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, maintain data security
  • Communication: Send account-related notifications, app updates (marketing only with explicit consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Database storage is encrypted using AES-256
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing assessments and updates
  • Incident Response: Procedures in place for potential security breaches

7. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Data Portability: Download your data in a machine-readable format

7.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain data processing activities

7.3 Control and Limitation

  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for app functionality
  • Processing Limitation: Request restrictions on how we process your data
  • Marketing Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-App Controls: Profile → Privacy Settings → Manage Data
  • Data Export: Profile → Account Settings → Export My Data
  • Account Deletion: Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account
  • Email Requests: Contact chordcrackhelp@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We respond to requests within 30 days (sooner when possible)

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account remains active and functional
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 3 years may be archived or deleted
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained for app improvement

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

ChordCrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will:

  • Delete the information immediately
  • Notify the child's parent or guardian if contact information is available
  • Suspend the account until proper parental consent is obtained

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at chordcrackhelp@gmail.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States and European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contracts for international transfers
  • Service Provider Agreements: Requiring third parties to meet our data protection standards

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: Categories of personal information collected and sources
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising CCPA rights

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in:

  • Our data practices
  • Legal requirements
  • App features and functionality

Notification Process:

  • Major Changes: 30-day advance notice via email and in-app notification
  • Minor Updates: Updated effective date and notice in app
  • Continued Use: Using the app after changes indicates acceptance

13. Cookie Policy

ChordCrack uses local storage and cookies for:

  • Authentication: Keeping you signed in securely
  • Preferences: Saving your app settings
  • Performance: Caching data for faster loading

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.
