Frequently Asked Questions

How does ChordCrack work? ‚ñº

ChordCrack plays guitar chord sounds, and you identify them by ear. The app uses a scientifically-designed progression system that starts with basic chords and gradually introduces more complex variations. Each correct answer earns you points and XP, while the hint system ensures you're always learning, never stuck.

Do I need a guitar to use ChordCrack? ‚ñº

No! ChordCrack is designed for ear training, not guitar playing. All you need is your device and headphones (recommended for the best experience). The app trains your ability to recognize chords by sound alone, which will improve your overall musicianship.

What skill level is ChordCrack for? ‚ñº

ChordCrack adapts to all skill levels. Beginners start with major and minor chords, intermediate players work on barre and power chords, and advanced users can tackle complex jazz voicings and chord progressions. The app automatically adjusts difficulty based on your performance.

How do the Daily Challenges work? ‚ñº

Daily Challenges consist of 5 quick rounds featuring basic chords. Complete them each day to maintain your streak and earn bonus XP. They're designed to take just 2-3 minutes, making it easy to maintain consistent practice even on busy days.

What are Practice Modes? ‚ñº

Practice Modes let you focus on specific chord types: Power Chords for rock fundamentals, Barre Chords for advanced patterns, Blues Chords for 7ths and extensions, or Mixed Mode for all chord types. Each mode tracks separate statistics so you can identify your strengths and areas for improvement.

How does the hint system work? ‚ñº

Our progressive hint system reveals information gradually: first slower playback, then individual string sounds, followed by jumbled finger positions, and finally partial chord diagrams. Each hint costs a small amount of points, encouraging you to try without help first while ensuring you're never completely stuck.

Can I play with friends? ‚ñº

Yes! Add friends using their username or invite code, compare stats, see their progress on the leaderboard, and motivate each other to improve. You can also see global rankings and compete with players worldwide.

Is my progress saved? ‚ñº

All your progress is automatically saved to the cloud when you're logged in. This means you can switch between devices and your stats, achievements, and progress will sync seamlessly. You can also export your data anytime from the settings menu.

How do I cancel my subscription? ‚ñº

Subscriptions can be managed through your device's app store settings. On iOS, go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions. On Android, open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions. Select ChordCrack and choose to cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

What if I need help or find a bug? ‚ñº

We're here to help! Email us at support@chordcrack.com with any questions, feedback, or bug reports. Include your device type and app version for technical issues. We typically respond within 24-48 hours and genuinely appreciate all feedback to improve ChordCrack.
